# Vapi-AI-Receptionist-Agentic-AI-
It serves as the intelligent front desk for inbound calls, helping businesses answer every call, understand why callers are calling, and take action without relying on traditional phone trees or voicemail. Unlike legacy IVRs that force callers through rigid menus, AI Receptionist uses natural language conversations.


**Darshit Sharma**  

A cutting-edge AI-powered virtual receptionist designed for businesses and call centers. This system leverages **agentic AI capabilities** to handle incoming calls, provide real-time responses, and manage client interactions efficiently. The platform supports monetization through per-minute billing for call handling.

---

## Features

- **AI-Powered Call Handling**: Automatically answers and responds to client calls using advanced NLP.
- **Agentic AI Capabilities**: Can make decisions, respond contextually, and escalate issues to humans if needed.
- **Monetization**: Tracks call durations and calculates charges per minute for paid interactions.
- **Real-Time Dashboard**: View active calls, revenue metrics, client interactions, and AI performance.
- **Extensible Architecture**: Easily integrate with telephony APIs, CRM systems, and other enterprise tools.
- **Logging & Analytics**: Records all calls, generates detailed analytics, and maintains a history for audits.
